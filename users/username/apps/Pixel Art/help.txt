PIXEL ART HELP
---

You may click one of the buttons on the
side bar, or the keyboard shortcut
denoted with square brackets "[]".

Save will export the image to
"tmp/export.png"

New will prompt for resolution. Close
that prompt to cancel.

Color and size set properties of the
brush.

Pen, line, fill, rect, and circle all
represent different drawing modes.

Press 'fullscreen' to toggle fullscreen
mode.
